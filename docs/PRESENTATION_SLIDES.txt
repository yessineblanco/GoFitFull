SLIDE 2 — CONTEXTE GENERAL

Title: Internship Context and Project Origin

- This project is carried out as a final-year internship (PFE) within Antigone Creative Agency, from February to June 2026.
- The internship focuses on designing and developing a complete fitness platform from specification to deployment.
- The project was initiated based on a real market observation: most local gyms and independent coaches lack any digital tool to manage their activity, track clients, or deliver programs online.
- GoFit was born from the need to bridge this gap by providing a modern, unified digital solution for both fitness users and coaching professionals.
- The internship combines hands-on software engineering with structured academic methodology under professional supervision.


SLIDE 3 — PROBLEMATIQUE
------------------------
Title: The Problem GoFit Addresses

- Most gyms and fitness spaces operate without any dedicated application or digital management system.
- Coaches rely on scattered tools (WhatsApp, spreadsheets, paper) to manage clients, schedules, and payments — creating inefficiency and poor follow-up.
- Users who want to train seriously have no single platform to plan workouts, track body progress with precision, and connect with a professional coach.
- There is no integrated AI-powered body measurement tool available to everyday fitness users, forcing manual and inaccurate tracking.
- The fitness industry — especially at the local and independent level — needs an innovative, all-in-one solution that serves users, coaches, and administrators in one connected experience.


SLIDE 4 — ETUDE DE L'EXISTANT
------------------------------
Title: What Already Exists and Where It Falls Short

- Consumer fitness apps (MyFitnessPal, Hevy, Strong) focus on workout logging and basic tracking, but offer no coaching workflow or marketplace.
- Coach-oriented tools exist as separate products for scheduling, payment, and communication — none unify the full coaching lifecycle.
- No widely available platform combines AI body-measurement analysis, workout planning, coach booking, messaging, and payment in one product.
- Admin/back-office tools in the fitness space are generic and not designed for gym or coaching business logic.
- Conclusion: existing solutions are either user-only or coach-only, and none deliver a seamless end-to-end experience connecting all three roles (client, coach, admin).


SLIDE 5 — SOLUTION PROPOSEE AVEC VALEUR AJOUTEE
-------------------------------------------------
Title: GoFit — The Proposed Solution

- GoFit is a unified fitness platform with a cross-platform mobile app (clients and coaches) and a web admin panel (administrators).
- For users: personalized workout planner, exercise library, progress tracking with AI body measurements, coach discovery and booking.
- For coaches: client management dashboard, program delivery, session scheduling, messaging, and revenue/pack tracking.
- For administrators: centralized user/coach management, content moderation, analytics, and operational oversight.
- Added value: GoFit eliminates tool fragmentation by combining training, coaching, communication, payment, and administration into one product — with role-based access and real-time capabilities.


SLIDE 6 — METHODOLOGIE DE TRAVAIL
-----------------------------------
Title: Methodology: Scrum Framework

- The project follows the Scrum framework with fixed 2-week sprints and incremental delivery of working features.
- Scrum roles applied: Product Owner (backlog priority), Scrum Master (process facilitation), Developer (design, build, test).
- Scrum ceremonies followed each sprint: Sprint Planning, Daily Scrum, Sprint Review, and Sprint Retrospective.
- Each sprint targets a potentially shippable increment validated against a clear Definition of Done.
- Backlog refinement is continuous — priorities adapt based on stakeholder feedback and technical discovery throughout the project lifecycle.


SLIDE 7 — TECHNOLOGIES A UTILISER
-----------------------------------
Title: Technology Stack

- Mobile Development: React Native + Expo for cross-platform iOS/Android delivery, with TypeScript and StyleSheet for type safety and styling.
- Admin Web Panel: Next.js (App Router) with Tailwind CSS and shadcn/ui for a modern, responsive admin interface.
- Backend and Database: Supabase providing PostgreSQL database, authentication (GoTrue), file storage, and real-time subscriptions.
- Real-Time Communication: Socket.io for live messaging and WebRTC for video call capabilities between coaches and clients.
- AI and Computer Vision: MediaPipe for automated body-measurement analysis from user photos, replacing manual input with intelligent tracking.


SLIDE 8 — PLANNING DE TRAVAIL
-------------------------------
Title: Work Plan and Sprint Roadmap

- Execution is structured into 13 two-week sprints covering the full project lifecycle.
- Phase 1 (Sprint 1-2): Requirements specification, UI/UX design, project setup, and authentication.
- Phase 2 (Sprint 3-5): Workout core, user profile, planner/calendar, exercise library, and progress tracking.
- Phase 3 (Sprint 6-9): AI body measurements (MediaPipe), coach onboarding, marketplace/packs, messaging, and video sessions.
- Phase 4 (Sprint 10-13): Admin panel development, end-to-end testing, performance optimization, bug fixing, and store/release preparation.
